🔄 System Logic & Integration Strategy
Renewables (Solar/Wind): Reduce the load on thermal generation, saving fossil fuels.
Hydropower: Regulates grid variations and acts as the system's main axis.
Thermal & Backup: Activates when national demand increases or hydropower drops (El Niño).
Nuclear: Proposed modern reactor for 24/7 continuous baseload generation.
